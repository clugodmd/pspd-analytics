Privacy Policy

PSPD Control Tower — Puget Sound Pediatric Dentistry

Puget Sound Pediatric Dentistry, PLLC (“PSPD,” “we,” “us,” or “our”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how the PSPD Control Tower application (“the Application”) collects, uses, and protects information when you connect your QuickBooks Online account.

1. Information We Collect

When you connect your QuickBooks Online account, the Application accesses the following data through Intuit’s API:

Data TypePurpose
Profit & Loss reportsRevenue and expense analytics
Balance Sheet reportsAsset and liability tracking
Cash Flow statementsCash position monitoring
Accounts Receivable agingOutstanding invoice tracking
Accounts Payable agingOutstanding bill tracking
Company informationAccount identification

We do not collect personal information about individual customers, employees, or patients from your QuickBooks account. We access only aggregated financial reports.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To generate read-only financial analytics dashboards for internal business use.
  • To calculate key performance indicators (KPIs) such as revenue trends, expense ratios, and cash flow metrics.
  • To store aggregated financial summaries for historical comparison.

3. Data Storage and Security

  • Financial data is stored in aggregated, summarized form — not raw transaction-level detail.
  • Data is stored using encrypted connections and access-controlled repositories.
  • OAuth 2.0 refresh tokens are stored as encrypted secrets and are never exposed in application code or logs.
  • The Application runs on secured infrastructure (GitHub Actions) with no persistent server.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, lease, or share your financial data with any third parties. Your data is used exclusively for internal PSPD business analytics.

5. Data Retention

Aggregated financial summaries are retained for as long as the Application is connected. Upon disconnection, stored data may be retained for up to 90 days for operational continuity, after which it will be deleted.

6. Your Rights

  • Disconnect: You may revoke the Application’s access at any time through QuickBooks Online under “Manage connected apps.”
  • Data Deletion: You may request deletion of all stored data by contacting us at drlugo@pugetsoundpd.com.
  • Access: You may request a copy of the data the Application has stored about your company.

7. Third-Party Services

The Application integrates with:

  • Intuit QuickBooks Online API: For financial data retrieval. Subject to Intuit’s Privacy Policy.
  • GitHub Pages: For dashboard hosting. Subject to GitHub’s Privacy Statement.

8. Children’s Privacy

The Application is a business analytics tool and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of the Application after changes constitutes acceptance.
