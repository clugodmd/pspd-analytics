SQL View Architecture (rpt.vw_income_allocation v3.1)
3-Part UNION ALL View:

Part 1 — CREATEDON (99.7% of rows)
  Period assigned by CREATEDON date. Excludes rows where MODIFIEDON moves them out of period.
  Filters: AMOUNT <> 0, LTYPE IN ('I','P') + AR/ADJ codes, excludes PP001/PP018
  Excludes: AINSX/PP014/PP005 with NULL proc code (orphan adjustments)

Part 2 — PROC_DOS (0.16% of rows, PP012 & PP015 only)
  Pre-paid allocations assigned by procedure date-of-service (not CREATEDON).
  Only included when CREATEDON falls BEFORE the period start.
  NOT EXISTS check prevents double-counting with Part 1.

Part 3 — MODIFIEDON (0.10% of rows, 4 codes only)
  Reprocessed payments assigned by MODIFIEDON date.
  LIMITED TO: PP017, PP014, PP012, AINSX
  PP008 excluded (111 junk MODIFIEDON rows = $4,332 overcounting).
  PF002 excluded (5 rows = $42 overcounting).
  NOT EXISTS checks prevent overlap with Parts 1 & 2.
Doctor Pay Calculation:

Collections = SUM(alloc_amount) per provider per office per period
X-Ray Amount = Collections from x-ray procedure codes
Adjusted Collections = Collections - X-Ray Amount
Pay = Adjusted Collections × Pay Schedule %
